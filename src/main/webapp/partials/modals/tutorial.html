Welcome to Exeo!

Exeo is a file sharing platform that let's devices transfer files between each other


To begin
  1. open Exeo on your other device
  2. copy your pair code into the pair box on the other device

You can then send and receive files between the two devices.
Please note, Exeo uses peer-to-peer communication which may not work in all situations.

Thank you for using Exeo and Enjoy!
